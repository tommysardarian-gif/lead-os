# LEAD 101 Path

## LEAD Acronym
Learn → Evaluate → Anticipate → Demonstrate

## Core Course
LEAD 101: Applied Self-Leadership & Communication

## Core Promise
Students learn to lead themselves by improving how they think, speak, decide, respond, and repeat daily systems.

## Phase 1: Communication
This is the first course. Collaboration and Contribution come later.

## Course Spine
1. Baseline Assessment
2. Identity and Maturity
3. Words, Beliefs, and Decisions
4. Emotional Intelligence
5. Night Routine and Sleep
6. Morning Routine
7. Daily Execution
8. Boundaries and Self-Parenting
9. Asking Better Questions
10. Communication and Integrity
11. 28-Day LEAD Challenge
12. Final Growth Presentation

## Key Principle
Understanding is mental. Knowing is experiential.
